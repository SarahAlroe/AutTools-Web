# A short introduction to the AutCorders

AutCorders are little photo and audio recording devices that have been built to integrate into peoples daily practice as simply and unobtrusively as possible. Having nothing but a single shutter button, you point the device at something to remember and press the button to take a picture. If you keep the button held, it will start recording audio instead until the button is let go. Neither pictures or audio recordings are of particularly high quality (although they carry a certain lo-fi aesthetic), but are well sufficient for evoking memories and thoughts from when they were made. 
Depending on how you choose to configure the AutCorder, it can either function as a regular (offline) digital camera and audio recorder, or as a wifi connected device, capable of transcribing recordings and sending them to a Discord or Telegram chat.
